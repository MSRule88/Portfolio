About Me

Freelance Web developer/IoT and Low Voltage system designer. Currently continuing my education to be a Software Integration Engineer or Embedded Systems Developer. My goal is to use the 20+ years of experience as an Electronics System Technician, with my current education path, to not only build the physical circuitry and hardware but also create software to integrate with it to build complete IoT and low voltage systems.

From a young age I have been intruiged with Computers and Electronics, I started programming with a TRS-80 from Radio Shack and loved to tinker with electronics as a child. This grew into a career as an Electronics Technician for the last 25+ years. I have taught myself just enough programming over the years to complete specific projects I was working on but recently took courses to become a certified programmer in order to make a career change. I have been sucessful in building my own Home Automation system as well as a few other applications. Growing up in Florida, I enjoy the beach and fresh seafood. Lover of all types of music but in recent years I have aquired a taste for Bluegrass and Jam Bands. I consider myself somewhat artistic and even sell some of my creations!
